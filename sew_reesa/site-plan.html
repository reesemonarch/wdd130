Purpose

The purpose of this website is to make available to potential customers all of my pricing lists, and contact information for customized work. Classes and tutorials will also be offered along with different levels of sewing kits.

My intended audience for my business is all seamstresses, quilters, women and men who need alterations, and children who are seeking to learn how to sew.
