Overview

Purpose

The purpose for this site is to build revenue in my new white water rafting business. We are currently a brick and mortar company only. We are using this site to expand our audience and make it easier for our customers to reserve equipment and navigate all the products that we have to offer.

Audience

Our target customers are middle to upper class clients. The products we provide are mostly geared toward teenager and older groups. We do have some products available for family outings, but the majority of our clientele tend to be the former described. They are coming to our site to find equipment rentals and to book our guides for their trips. Currently, customer needs in our area can only be met by coming into our location or through phonecalls. The website will lighten the booking load on our staff so they can better attend to guests in person. While we would like customers to be able to access and navigate our site effectively from all sources, we are primarily concerned with cell phone access. We see this as being our customer's primary mode of booking. If the site is as successful as we hope, we plan to develop an app.
