About Us That’s Sew Reesa Boutique has been a labor of love over many years of hard work. It is owned and managed by Theresa Thompson. Theresa has a B.S. in Family and Consumer Science Education with over 7 years of teaching experience. Her favorite aspects of the business are the design process and seeing her creations enjoyed by customers and their families. She strives to keep a 5-star rating for all products and works tirelessly to ensure customers get the best quality products available on the market. Always going above and beyond the industry standard, Theresa’s creations withstand the test of time.
